Tramlines

About

Tramlines is an annual free music festival held in Sheffield. Inspired by the city's tram network, the festival runs over 70 venues and 4 main stages in the city centre and has been running since 2009.

Why participate?

The festival is organised by local venue owners, promoters and volunteers. By volunteering at Tramlines, you can help a fun organisation in your local community while meeting new people and listening to amazing music. In 2011, tramlines won the 'Best Metropolitan Festival' uk festival award, this emphasises the success of Tramlines festival.

Outlines

In 2016, Tramlines organisers launched a new one-day annual event; Outlines Festival which is mainly dedicated to supporting new bands
