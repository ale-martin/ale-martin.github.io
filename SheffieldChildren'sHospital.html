Sheffield Children's Hospital Charity

About

Donations are used to help improve the Children's hospital by buying life-saving equipment, funding critical research and creating a comfortable and friendly environment for the children.

Research

Charitable donations fund vital research not covered by the NHS - each year over £250,000 of research is carried out. A brand new clinical research facility will be opened in the near future, which is the first of its kind in the UK.

Facilities

Make it Better appeal is the biggest campaign to date and will see the hospital renovated with spacious wards and departments, with lots of private ensuite rooms allowing families to stay together. Also provides secure outdoor space for relaxation.

Room sponsorship is helpful for patients to provide an environment catered especially for them. Consultation rooms can be fitted with additions that go beyond basic NHS provisions such as enhanced parent facilities, lighting and equipment.

Environment

This charity makes play specialists jobs easier by funding craft materials, toys and games allowing children to play and participate in activities while at hospital. Each year £5000 needs to be raised to donate to the team to continue their amazing work.

Equipment

Current equipment which is 10 years out of date, can be replaced with a brand new Multipurpose Fluroscopy System, enabling quick diagnosis and surgical intervention.

How you can help

  • £50 would pay for colourful plants in the patient sensory garden provides a relaxing environment for children and their families.
  • £500 would pay for furnishings inside playrooms.
  • £1,000 could help furnish parents rooms, provide sofas and a relaxing area while loved ones are in care.
  • £25,000 could sponsor a room such as an ensuite bedroom or a physiotherapy room in the new building.
